# PRESENTATION DELIVERY TIPS

## Before You Present:

1. **Customize with Real Numbers**
   - Insert your actual case studies if available
   - Add specific financial metrics for your region
   - Include local currency and cost examples

2. **Tell Your Origin Story** (2-3 min)
   - Why you built Thutonet
   - What problem inspired you
   - Your commitment to African education

3. **Practice Transitions**
   - Slide 2 → 3: "Now, what does this cost?"
   - Slide 7 → 8: "But attendance is even more important..."
   - Slide 14 → 15: "Let me show you what this looks like in practice..."

4. **Use Real Screenshots**
   - Replace generic descriptions with actual product screenshots
   - Demo the dashboard during presentation
   - Show real student data (anonymized)

5. **Time Allocation (60-minute presentation)**
   - Slides 1-3 (The Problem): 5 min
   - Slides 4-12 (Solutions): 20 min
   - Slides 13-15 (Implementation): 10 min
   - Slides 16-18 (Value Proposition): 10 min
   - Slides 19-22 (Next Steps): 10 min
   - Slides 23-24 (Closing & Q&A): 5 min

## During Your Presentation:

1. **Tell Stories, Not Facts**
   - Every statistic needs a story
   - "Mary teaches at a school with 500 students..."
   - "Ahmed's parents never knew his attendance was at 60%..."

2. **Show Emotion**
   - Care deeply about the problem
   - Be enthusiastic about the solution
   - Show passion for African education

3. **Interactive Elements**
   - Ask rhetorical questions: "How many of you use 5+ different systems?"
   - Pause for effect after key statistics
   - Engage the audience: "Who here has experience with paper-based records?"

4. **Use the Rule of Three**
   - Frame benefits in threes: "Organized. Transparent. Improving."
   - Problem sets in threes: "Teachers are burnt out. Parents are disconnected. No one has data."

5. **End Strong**
   - Memorable final statement
   - Clear call to action
   - Emotional resonance

## After Your Presentation:

1. **Follow Up Within 24 Hours**
   - Send presentation slides
   - Include pricing details
   - Offer demo booking

2. **Customize Next Conversation**
   - If they asked about cost: Lead with ROI
   - If they asked about security: Lead with compliance
   - If they asked about implementation: Lead with timeline

3. **Build Relationship**
   - Monthly check-in emails
   - Share relevant case studies
   - Educational content about the problems they're solving
