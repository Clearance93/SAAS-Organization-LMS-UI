## 🚨 The Problem: A Crisis in Educational Administration

### The Current Reality

Across Africa, educational institutions, churches, and NGOs are drowning in administrative chaos:

#### **Problem 1: Paper-Based Nightmare**
**The Situation:**
- Schools still use physical registers for attendance
- Report cards are handwritten or typed individually
- Student records stored in filing cabinets
- Lost documents, damaged files, and missing information

**Real Impact:**
- 📉 **30% of student records** contain errors or are incomplete
- ⏰ **3-5 days** to generate report cards for one class
- 💰 **$12,000/year** spent on paper, printing, and storage
- 😰 **15 hours/week** wasted on manual data entry
